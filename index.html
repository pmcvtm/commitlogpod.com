The Commit Log: a new podcast

When it's time to share their work, developers make a commit to a repository which contains everyone's changes in succession. Together these commits tell a story about who's involved, when they're working, and what they were thinking. When it's time to hear that shared story, other developers can look at The Commit Log to the scoop.

Host Patrick McVeety-Mill interviews guests about their workday: what are they doing, when are they doing it, who they do it with, and what hurts and helps throughout it.
